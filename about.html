Who We Are

Established in 1993, VVJM Paramahamsa Vidyalayam offers holistic education from Pre-KG to XII. We foster academic excellence, creativity, and moral values for building confident, responsible citizens.

Motto: "Man Making and Nation Building"

Our Vision

To develop responsible, knowledgeable, and morally upright citizens who positively contribute to society.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education balancing academics, values, and life skills, nurturing leadership and creativity in every student.

Understanding the 3Ds DUTY • DEVOTION • DISCIPLINE

DUTY (DHARMA)

Represents the responsibilities and obligations one has towards society, family, and work, which should be performed with selfless zeal and faithfulness. 

DEVOTION (BHAKTI)

Signifies intense love and loyalty, particularly a deep, steady connection to God or a spiritual ideal. 

DISCIPLINE (VAIRAGYA)

Refers to the internal self-control, adherence to rules, and orderly conduct necessary to regulate all aspects of one's life and keep the other two "D's" in balance.
