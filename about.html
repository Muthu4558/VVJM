Who We Are

Established in 04/07/1993, VVJM Paramahamsa Vidyalayam offers holistic education from Pre-KG, LKG, UKG, I to XII. We foster academic excellence, creativity, and moral Values for building confident, responsible citizens.

Motto: "Man Making and Nation Building"

Our Vision

To develop responsible, knowledgeable, and morally upright citizens who positively contribute to welfare of the Nation and Society their in.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education balancing the academics age old Values, and life skills, nurturing leadership and creativity in every student.

Understanding the 3Ds DUTY • DEVOTION • DISCIPLINE

DUTY (DHARMA)

Represents the responsibilities and obligations one has towards this society, family, and work, which should be performed with selfless zeal and faithfulness. 

DEVOTION (BHAKTI)

Signifies intense love and loyalty, particularly a deep, steady connection to God or a spiritual ideal. 

DISCIPLINE (VAIRAGYA)

Refers to the internal self-control, adherence to rules, and orderly conduct necessary to regulate all aspects of one's life and keep the other two "D's" in balance.

Inspirational Visit by Dignitaries

On 13th October 2025 (Monday, 9:45 a.m), our school was honored by the visit of distinguished guests who shared their wisdom and motivation with our students through an inspiring interactive session.

We had the privilege of welcoming:

  • Dr. Krish Soundrarajan, F.R.C.S., M.S., M.B.A — Cardiovascular Surgeon, Director of SAINOI Hospitals, New York (U.S.A), Chairman of South Asia Cardiovascular Surgeons Federation, and Director overseeing cardiovascular surgeries in New York.
  • Smt. Susheela Ramaraj, Leading Advocate, Canada.
  • Smt. Chithra Yaswant, M.S (Dietician), U.S.A.

The dignitaries inspired our students through their motivational speeches and shared their global experiences. The children enthusiastically participated in an interactive session, asking questions and gaining valuable insights.

This memorable visit was a true inspiration for the students of VVJM School. The dignitaries’ kind words and encouragement motivated every child to dream big and work hard towards their goals.
