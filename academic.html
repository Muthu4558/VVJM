Our Academics

At VVJM Paramahamsa Vidyalayam, we provide a structured and holistic academic journey that nurtures curiosity, builds confidence, and prepares students for life with strong values and intellectual excellence.

Pre-Primary (Nursery – UKG)

Our Pre-Primary program introduces children to the Joy of Learning through Fun-filled Activities, Storytelling, Art, and Play. The focus is on building Language Skills, Early Numeracy, Social-Interaction, and Emotional Growth in a warm and Caring Environment.

Primary (Grades I – V)

The Primary stage builds on foundational learning with an emphasis on language, mathematics, science, and moral education. Our child-centered approach encourages curiosity and self-expression while developing discipline and teamwork through engaging activities.

Middle (Grades VI – VIII)

The Middle School curriculum strengthens academic understanding and introduces higher-order concepts. Students develop analytical and problem-solving skills through project-based learning, experiments, and digital resources. Co-curricular activities enhance creativity and confidence.

Senior Secondary (Grades IX – XII)

At the Senior Secondary level, we prepare students for board examinations and future careers through specialized streams, mentoring, and career guidance. The program emphasizes conceptual clarity, leadership, and life skills to help students thrive in higher education and beyond.
