About VVJM Paramahamsa Vidyalayam

Established in 1993, VVJM Paramahamsa Vidyalayam is a leading educational institution in Tiruchirappalli, dedicated to providing value-based quality education. We offer a comprehensive curriculum for students from Pre-KG through to Higher Secondary, guided by the ideals of Swami Vivekananda and cared for by highly qualified teachers.

Our school shapes the younger generation with a strong foundation in academics, values, and discipline, ensuring holistic growth and preparation for meeting future challenges.

Golden Rules for Living

Simple principles to live with respect, responsibility, and integrity.

  • 1. If you open it, close it.
  • 2. If you turn it on, turn it off.
  • 3. If you unlock it, lock it up.
  • 4. If you break it, admit it.
  • 5. If you can’t fix it, call in someone who can.
  • 6. If you borrow it, return it.
  • 7. If you value it, take care of it.
  • 8. If you make a mess, clean it up.
  • 9. If you move it, put it back.
  • 10. If it belongs to someone else, get permission to use it.
  • 11. If you don’t know how to operate it, leave it alone.
  • 12. If it’s none of your business, don’t ask questions.
